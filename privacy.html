Inbox4 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Inbox4 processes all photos entirely on your device. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or any third party.

Camera

The camera is used only to take your photos within the app. Camera frames are processed on-device and are never uploaded.

Photo Library

Photo Library access is used to save your finished photo strip and to let you pick your own custom backgrounds. These images stay on your device.

Data Collection

Inbox4 contains no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. No data is collected.
